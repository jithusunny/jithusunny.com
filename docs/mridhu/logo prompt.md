Create a **minimal, elegant wordmark logo** for a premium Indian home & lifestyle brand named **“Mridhu by Elsa”**.

### Composition & Layout
- Horizontal (landscape) logo
- Text tightly cropped with **very minimal padding**
- Logo fills most of the canvas
- Transparent background (PNG with alpha)

### Typography
- **“Mridhu”**
  - Handwritten calligraphic script
  - Soft, flowing curves
  - Medium-weight strokes (slightly thicker, not thin)
  - Feminine, warm, calm
- **“BY ELSA”**
  - Small uppercase
  - Clean modern sans-serif
  - Centered below “Mridhu”
  - Light letter spacing

### Graphic Element
- Small, delicate botanical motif (leaf / twig / floral stem)
- Subtly integrated above or flowing along the curves of “Mridhu”
- Hand-drawn feel
- Decorative, not dominant

### Color Palette
- Primary text: muted antique gold (`#C8A24D` or slightly darker)
- Subtext: slightly darker gold or brown
- Flat color only (no gradients)

### Style Constraints
- Flat design
- No background
- No shadows, glow, or 3D effects
- No mockups or textures
- Clean, minimal, premium

### Output Requirements
- Transparent PNG
- Clean edges
- Suitable for website header, Shopify logo, and Instagram profile
- Balanced and readable at small and large sizes
