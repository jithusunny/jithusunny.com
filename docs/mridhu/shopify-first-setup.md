

# Shopify First-Sale Checklist (Compressed)

> Goal: enable 1 real order. Nothing extra.

---

## DONE
- Shopify account created
- Razorpay enabled
- mridhu.in connected


---

## 1. STORE BASICS
Settings → Store details
- Store name: Mridhu by Elsa
- Contact + sender email: mridhubyelsa@gmail.com
- Address: real address
- Currency: INR | Timezone: India

---

## 2. THEME
Online Store → Themes
- Publish Dawn (or Sense)

Customize (only):
- Header: text logo or logo image
- Homepage: keep
  - Image banner (1)
  - Featured product
  - Short brand text
- Remove sliders, extras
- Footer: email only

---

## 3. NAVIGATION
Online Store → Navigation
- Main menu: Home, Shop (All products)
- Footer menu: Policies only

---

## 4. POLICIES (REQUIRED)
Settings → Policies
- Generate: Privacy, Refund, Shipping, Terms
- Edit lightly (shipping timeline, refunds)

---

## 5. PRODUCT (CORE)
Products → Add product
- Title: simple
- Description: size, fabric, what’s included, care
- Media: 4–6 photos (+ optional video)
- Price: real selling price
- Inventory: actual quantity
- Status: Active

---

## 6. COLLECTION
Products → Collections
- Create: Bedsheets
- Manual, add product
- Use on homepage

---

## 7. SHIPPING
Settings → Shipping
- India only
- Flat rate (₹99/₹149) OR free
- Remove international shipping

---

## 8. TAX
Settings → Taxes
- Enable GST only if registered
- Else leave off

---

## 9. CHECKOUT
Settings → Checkout
- Phone number required
- Auto capture payments ON

---

## 10. EMAIL
Settings → Notifications
- Check order confirmation
- Sender email correct
- Send test

---

## 11. TEST ORDER (MANDATORY)
- Place test order (₹1 or test mode)
- Verify: payment, order, email

---

## 12. FINAL CHECK
- Mobile view OK
- Add to cart works
- Checkout works
- Payment works
